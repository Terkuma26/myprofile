WELCOME TO BENUE ICT ECOSYSTEM

Benue ICT Ecosystem” initiative is designed to empower Benue State youths with the necessary skills and knowledge to excel and dominate in digital economy and foster economic growth and innovation within the state. THE INITIATIVE: The “Benue ICT Ecosystem” programme holds immense importance in today's digital age.
As technology evolves, the industries shape the job markets. It is crucial for Benue youths to be equipped with the skills
required to thrive in a digital environment. By providing specialized training in digital
marketing & e-commerce, advanced data analytics, data science & analysis,
UI/UX, frontend development, and backend development.
The training is aimed at bridging the digital skills gap in Benue State and to ensure that the youths are well-prepared for the future of work.
This initiative aligns with the government’s vision of a digitally
empowered Benue State and supports the Sustainable Development Goals of quality education (SDG 4)
and decent work and economic growth (SDG 8).
